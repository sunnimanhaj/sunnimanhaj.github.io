Celebrating the birthday of the Prophet ﷺ was not done by the most virtuous generation after the Prophet ﷺ who were his companions. They did not use to distinguish it from other nights with any act of worship which was something they learnt from the Prophet ﷺ who did not use to worship his Lord except according to what He revealed to him.
