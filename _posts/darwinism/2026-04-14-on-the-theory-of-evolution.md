The theory of evolution, known as Darwinism, contradicts the Book of Allah, the Sunnah of His Messenger (ﷺ), and the consensus of the people of knowledge and faith (i.e., scholars). It is established in the Quran and the Sunnah that Adam was created from dust, and that his wife, Eve (Hawa), was created from him.
